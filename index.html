AKAN NAMES

.In Africa naming of children is quit a major ceremony. Children are mostly named after the ancestors they replace or even their grandparents.some are even named according to the present occurrences.For instance the weather or even a major event. The Akan people of Ghana happen to name their children according to the day they were born

Here are the Akan Names

Male Names

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

Female Names

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
